(popularly known as “Abchi”) was incorporated in 2021 by two technical professionals who are friends from their engineering college days, but have come from very different professional backgrounds. Since then, he has proven his mettle in entrepreneurship by successfully creating a leading brand in e-Governance & IT Services Sectors.
